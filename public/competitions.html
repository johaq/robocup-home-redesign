Competitions
Compete in @Home.

RoboCup@Home takes place at the annual RoboCup World Championship event as well as a growing number of regional competitions throughout the year. All regional competitions are open to international teams. New teams are highly encouraged to pick a regional competition as their first event. For all teams, a regional competition is the best way to prepare for the World Championship.
